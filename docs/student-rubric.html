What Counts as a Credible Reference?

Course lecture slides count as one reference regardless of how many slide decks are cited.

Credible

  • Peer-reviewed journal articles
  • Textbooks (incl. Serjeant's Observational Cosmology)
  • NASA / ESA / JAXA official pages
  • ArXiv preprints
  • Course lecture notes / slides
  • PBS Space Time videos

Not Credible (on their own)

  • Wikipedia (OK only as supplementary)
  • Anime wiki pages
  • Personal blogs
  • AI-generated summaries
